ABOUT US

Our History

A kasi (township) chisanyama is a popular social venue in South Africa where people gather to grill meat, enjoy music, and socialize. The word "chisanyama" comes from Zulu meaning "burn meat". These venues grew from township culture and became vibrant community hubs.

Our Mission

We provide authentic South African braai cuisine, a warm atmosphere, and the highest quality meals. We aim to be a place where families and friends create memories while enjoying the true taste of Mzansi.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for authentic South African barbecue, offering an unforgettable food experience and exceptional customer service.
