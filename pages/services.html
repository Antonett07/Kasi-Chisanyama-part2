A Kasi Chisanyama is a type of informal, casual restaurant or food establisment that specializes in traditional South African cusine, practically braai (barbecued) meats and other local dishes.

We offer:

Braai Meats

  • Boerewors
  • Steak
  • Chicken
  • Pork

Traditional dishes

  • Pap
  • Mogodu
  • Chops
  • Morogo
  • Chakalaka
  • Side dishes

Cold beverages

  • Soft drinks
  • Water

Dining experience

  • Casual atmosphere: a relaxed, informal setting where customers can enjoy their meals
  • Outdoor seating: we offer outdoor seating areas

Takeaway and delivery

  • Takeaway: customers can order food to go and enjoy elsewhere
  • Delivery: we offer delivery services to close-by places with our own drivers

Catering Services

  • Braai packages: customizable braai packages for events and gatherings
