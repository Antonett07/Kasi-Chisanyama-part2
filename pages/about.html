ABOUT US:

Our History

A kasi (township) chisanyama is a popular social venue in South Africa where people buy and grill meat, often on a "buy-and-braai" basis, creating a vibrant community atmosphere. The term itself is a Zulu word meaning "burn meat". The history is rooted in the township culture, where these informal braai (barbecue) spots developed as a way for communities to gather, socialize, and enjoy grilled food in an accessible, communal setting.

Our Mission

We aim to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where friends and families can gather, indulge in delicious food, and create lasting memories. We are committed to sourcing the highest quality meats, spices, and ingredients, ensuring that each dish we serve is flavorful, satisfying, and prepared with passion. Through exceptional service and attention to detail, we strive to exceed our customers' expectations and become a destination of choice for those seeking a taste of South Africa.

Our vision:

To be the leading fast-food restaurant providing quality and authentic African cuisine to people around the world, To become the premier destination for authentic South African barbecue, renowned for its delectable dishes, vibrant atmosphere, and unparalleled customer satisfaction.
